Our Story

HIMABERB™ is produced by HPIC Pvt. Ltd. which was setup as a small family owned business in 1958 exporting various medicinal herbs and oils from the Himalayan mountain range internationally. We began manufacturing Berberine Hydrochloride (HIMABERB™) in 1968 and today, with over 60 years of technical expertise we are one of the leading exporters to Japan, South Korea, United States and Europe.
